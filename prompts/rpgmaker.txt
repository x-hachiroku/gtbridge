<Placeholders>
A placeholder may be marked with a backslash. It may be a variable or a control code.
Variables will get replaced during runtime, e.g.:
\\v[3] - 3rd general variable
\\i[4] - 4th icon
Control codes affect text display after it, e.g.:
\\c[1] - Change text color to color 1. Defaults to 0.
\\{ - Increase text size
Placeholders may be nested, e.g., `\\i[\\v[3]]`.
Do not modify a placeholder; Use it directly in the output text.
</Placeholders>
